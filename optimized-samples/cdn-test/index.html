Extra — lip-flip at higher WebP quality

Since q=82 lost 8.6% saturation, checked whether higher q recovers it:

  • WebP q=95 → 276.6 KB · Δsat −6.43%
  • WebP q=100 → 368.2 KB · Δsat −9.75%

Verdict: libwebp is spec-locked to 4:2:0 chroma; no WebP quality tier fixes it. AVIF q=80 through the CDN is also 4:2:0 and shows the same drop. The only path for saturation-sensitive images is to serve the original JPEG (bypass CDN transform for that allowlist).
