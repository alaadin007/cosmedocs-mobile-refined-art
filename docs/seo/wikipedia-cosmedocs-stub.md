'''Cosmedocs''' is a British [[aesthetic medicine]] clinic group headquartered on [[Harley Street]] in central London, with a second site in Buckinghamshire. It is led by medical director [[Ahmed Faraz Haq]], a published clinician whose work has appeared in the ''[[Journal of the American Academy of Dermatology]]'' and the ''[[Aesthetic Surgery Journal]]''.<ref name="jaad2022" /><ref name="asj2022" />

The clinic is doctor-led and focuses on non-surgical facial rejuvenation, including [[botulinum toxin]] treatments, [[hyaluronic acid]] dermal fillers, regenerative injectables such as [[polynucleotide]]s and [[Profhilo]], and energy-based skin therapies.

== Clinical activity ==
Cosmedocs operates from premises on Harley Street, London, the historical centre of British private medicine.<ref>{{cite web|title=Harley Street|publisher=Royal Borough of Westminster|url=https://www.westminster.gov.uk/}}</ref> [[Care Quality Commission]]–regulated procedures at the London site are delivered in partnership with PrivaDr Ltd, 10 Harley Street.
